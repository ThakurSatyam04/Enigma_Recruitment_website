TAG LINE - Where passion meets innovation


VISION - Humanity has been successful and prosperous on our planet palpably due to our ability to be curious and collaborate with other human beings. Our Enigma E-Cell encourages and aims to transform itself into a platform to launch the leaders of tomorrow towards the path of success. We plan to widen our scope of impact by allowing not only students but working professionals, retired personnel, etc. to have an opportunity to fabricate their vision into reality. Our E-cell encourages and enables the members to grow and gain exposure in the entrepreneurship realm by stepping out of their comfort zone for pursuits greater than themselves that are in general perceived to be impossible. We expose our members to various environments and circumstances with the intention that they develop their adaptability and decision-making skills which are imperative in exceling in the entrepreneurship world. We believe in improving one step a day in achieving our vision, as the ancient Chinese proverb goes “The Man who moves a mountain begins by carrying away small stones”. Our team is a group of hard-working individuals who work day in, day out to bring us closer to our vision, one step at a time.
